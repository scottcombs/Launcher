Launcher Help

Welcome to the Launcher help page. Launcher is a small productivity tool to help you get to your favorite applications, folders and files with hotkeys that work everywhere. Easily accessed from your menubar, Launcher runs quietly in the background waiting for you to request specific applications, folders or files to launch.
